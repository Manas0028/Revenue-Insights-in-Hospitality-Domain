# 🏨 Revenue Insights in Hospitality Domain

## 📌 Project Overview

AtliQ Grands, a luxury and business hotel chain, has been facing declining revenue and market share due to increasing competition.

This project uses Power BI to analyze hotel booking and revenue data and provide actionable insights that support strategic business decisions.

The dashboard helps stakeholders monitor key performance indicators such as Revenue, Occupancy %, ADR, RevPAR, Realisation %, and Cancellation Rate.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

AtliQ Grands wants to regain its competitive position in the hospitality market by leveraging data-driven decision-making.

Key questions addressed:

* Which hotels generate the highest revenue?
* Which cities perform best?
* How do occupancy and revenue trends change over time?
* What is the impact of cancellations on revenue?
* Which room categories contribute the most to business performance?

* Average Rating

## 🔍 Key Insights

### Revenue Performance

* Luxury hotels contribute the majority of total revenue.
* Revenue concentration is highest in major metropolitan locations.

### Occupancy Analysis

* Several hotels show high occupancy but comparatively lower revenue, indicating pricing optimization opportunities.

### Cancellation Trends

* Cancellation rates significantly impact realized revenue.
* Certain properties experience higher cancellation percentages than others.

### Customer Experience

* Hotels with stronger ratings generally achieve better occupancy and revenue performance.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

### 1. Dynamic Pricing

Adjust room pricing based on demand, seasonality, and occupancy patterns.

### 2. Reduce Cancellations

Implement targeted offers and flexible booking policies to improve realization rates.

### 3. Improve Underperforming Hotels

Focus on operational improvements and marketing initiatives for low-performing properties.

### 4. Enhance Guest Experience

Use customer feedback and ratings to improve service quality and increase repeat bookings.
